CORONA VIRUS

Coronary viral disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by coronary viruses that were recently discovered.

People suffering from COVID-19 experience mild to moderate symptoms and recover without special treatment.

HOW YOU SPREAD

The virus that causes COVID-19 is transmitted mainly through droplets that cause coughing, sneezing or shortness of breath in an infected person. These droplets are so heavy that they cannot float in the air, so they quickly fall to the floor or other parts.

You can be infected by inhaling the virus if you are in close contact with someone with COVID-19, or by touching the infected area and then touching your eyes, nose or mouth.

COVID-19 affects people in different ways. The vast majority of infected people have mild symptoms of the disease and recover without hospitalization.
